About Sawadee Dining Boat

Where Thai Cuisine Meets the Beauty of the Sea

Sawadee Dining Boat was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate food lovers and sailors who wanted to offer an unforgettable dining experience. Inspired by the vibrant flavors of Thailand and the serenity of the sea, our restaurant is more than just a meal—it’s a journey.

With an authentic Thai menu curated by top chefs, we invite you to enjoy a private 2-hour dining cruise, exploring the stunning waterfront while indulging in delicious, handcrafted dishes.

A Unique Dining Experience

What makes us special? At Sawadee Dining Boat, we believe in combining exquisite flavors with breathtaking views. Each trip is exclusive—meaning you and your guests will have the entire boat to yourselves.

  • 🚢 Private dining cruise: Perfect for couples, families, and small groups (4-20 guests).
  • 🌅 Scenic 2-hour trip: Enjoy the waterfront from sunrise to sunset.
  • 🍽️ Authentic Thai menu: Curated by renowned Thai chefs.
  • 🎉 Customizable events: Birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gatherings.
